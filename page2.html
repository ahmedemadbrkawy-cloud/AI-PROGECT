ARTIFICAL INTELLIGENCE

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a transformative branch of computer science dedicated to creating systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as reasoning, learning, problem-solving, and understanding language. By leveraging vast datasets and complex algorithms—specifically machine learning and deep learning—AI identifies patterns to make predictions or decisions with remarkable speed and precision. Today, AI is an integral part of daily life, powering virtual assistants, personalized recommendations, navigation apps, and medical diagnostic tools. While it offers significant benefits in efficiency and innovation, it also presents challenges, including ethical concerns, algorithmic bias, and potential job displacement. As this technology continues to evolve, its impact grows across industries, promising to reshape how we work and live
